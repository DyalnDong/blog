## B. 深度 thread 大纲 ×3(每周 1 篇,周六写)

### Thread 1 ·《The denominator trap》(来源:2026-05-14《大多数糟糕决定都输在分母上》)

> 这是你最强的差异化内容。把"分母思维"翻成普世决策框架。

- **Hook**: "I made $50k on a trade." That sentence contains zero information. Here's the math that finally fixed how I make decisions 🧵
- 1: Same profit, different denominator = completely different decision. Most bad calls aren't bad luck — they're a wrong denominator.
- 2: **Capital** as denominator → ROI. $50k on $1M is 5%. On $100k it's 50%. Always ask "on how much capital?" first.
- 3: **Time** as denominator → annualized. $50k in a month feels like genius. Annualize it honestly and the fantasy dies. (This is how I lost on a leveraged trade — extrapolated 3 great months into "1500% a year.")
- 4: **Max drawdown** as denominator → risk-adjusted return. A "3x" with an 80% drawdown along the way is almost impossible to repeat.
- 5: **Benchmark** as denominator → alpha. Made 5% while the index did 30%? Your "active management" destroyed value. Just buy the index.
- 6: **Opportunity cost** as denominator → real return. $50k vs the 4% you'd earn doing nothing = $10k of real edge, minus all the hours you spent. Often the hourly rate is embarrassing.
- 7: The hidden ones: "just $X a day" financing, 0.6%/month installments (really 13%+ APR). Swap the denominator to annualized % and the answer reveals itself.
- **Close**: My rule now: every money number, list 3 denominators before deciding. Slower. But way fewer dumb decisions. / CTA to newsletter.

### Thread 2 ·《Why the first 90% feels like failure》(来源:2025-06-05《被鲸鱼吞没》第04节 指数增长)

> 适合 build-in-public 受众,讲为什么早期没反馈也要坚持。

- **Hook**: Exponential growth looks like failure for 90% of the time. The lily pond doubles daily, covers the pond in 30 days — and on day 27 it's still only 1/8 covered. 🧵
- 1: Linear paths give feedback every step. Exponential paths give you almost nothing early. That's the trap.
- 2: Early = no signal. Middle = you waver. By the time it's obviously working, it's nearly over.
- 3: Most people quit in the no-feedback zone. Not because they're weak — because their judgment system is built for linear, not exponential.
- 4: Exponential only works if the thing has the right structure: low cost to make, low marginal cost, low distribution cost, broad use, long-term value.
- 5: Writing, knowledge, personal brand, systems — same shape. No early reward. Feels like shouting into the void.
- 6: Munger: "compound interest is the 8th wonder." The forgotten second half: never interrupt it unnecessarily.
- **Close**: The question isn't "should I keep going." It's "is this thing even shaped like a curve — and can I survive the flat part." / CTA.

### Thread 3 ·《I switched fully to Claude — and it stopped being a chatbot》(来源:2026-04-16《全面转投 Claude》)

> 支柱 ① 的代表作,讲 AI 工具如何重构工作流。

- **Hook**: I switched fully to Claude this month. Somewhere along the way it stopped being a chatbot and became an operating layer for my work. 4 layers 🧵
- 1: **Chat** — the base. The real upgrade is memory: it quietly builds a profile of how I work. (Like a Soul.md that writes itself.)
- 2: **Cowork** — it reaches into my actual files & tools. The killer feature: it lives inside Excel. I dropped a few hundred-thousand rows in and got an analyst-grade report.
- 3: **Claude Code** — give it a task, it reads, writes, runs, reads errors, fixes, repeats. And Plan Mode: it interviews YOU until your vague idea becomes a concrete spec. Like having a consultant before you build.
- 4: **Remote (Dispatch + Computer Use)** — assign work from my phone, it runs on my computer. Think of something on the commute; it's done by the time you arrive.
- **Close**: As a non-engineer, this is the first time the distance between "idea" and "shipped" felt small enough to cross. / CTA.

## C. 短推草稿 ×15(工作日每天 1 条;发布时删掉中文)

**Build with AI(支柱①)**

   _(Plan Mode 被低估了:不用想清楚就能开始,它会把你问到出方案。起步成本几乎为零。)_

3. The Comet browser plugin replaced a $20/mo tool for me overnight. Same job, plus it already knows my context. Hard to justify paying for the old thing.
   _(浏览器插件一夜之间替掉了我每月 $20 的工具。)_

4. Building in public update: [本周做的 App 进展一句话 + 截图]. What broke this week: [一个坑]. What I'd do differently: [一句].
   _(每周 build 进度模板:做了啥+截图、踩了啥坑、下次怎么改。)_

**Think in systems(支柱②)**

5. "I made $50k." That sentence has zero information. On how much capital? Over how long? With what drawdown? The denominator is the whole story.
   _(分母才是全部信息。)_

6. Floating profit isn't real until you realize it. If you don't take it off the table, it's just a number that hasn't happened yet.
   _(浮盈不兑现,就等于没发生过。)_

7. Stop saying "this'll probably work." Put a number on it. "60% it hits X within a month." The moment you're forced to write 60 vs 70, your brain actually thinks.
   _(别说"应该会",写个具体百分比,大脑才会真正停下来想。)_

8. Barbell strategy in one line: 85% locked in the safest thing, 15% on uncapped upside. Even if the 15% goes to zero, you lost 15%. One hit covers everything.
   _(杠铃策略:85% 锁死下行,15% 不封顶上行。)_

9. Compound interest's scary part isn't the 1%. It's the "every day." Short-term riches are rare because they crush the denominator (time) down to 1 — and at 1, there is no compounding.
   _(复利可怕的不是 1%,是"每天"。)_

10. Defense is offense. In a down market, not losing IS winning.
    _(防御就是进攻。)_

**Cross-border money × AI life + 失败复盘(支柱③)**

11. My most expensive lesson: I extrapolated 3 great months of a leveraged trade into "1500% annualized," sized up, and gave it all back on one pullback. The math was there. I ignored the denominator.
    _(最贵的教训:把 3 个月的运气当成 1500% 年化,加杠杆,一个回调全吐回去。)_

12. I lost most of a heavily-weighted NFT position in '22. Not because NFTs are evil — because I sized it on a fantasy annualized return. The asset wasn't the mistake. The denominator was.
    _(22 年重仓 NFT 亏掉,错不在 NFT,错在我用幻想的年化去定仓位。)_

13. I run my own money like a one-man research desk now: AI for data cleaning, scripts to verify my own thesis, written probabilities I grade later. Retail with an institutional process.
    _(我把理财做成了"个人版机构投研部"。)_

14. The "just $X a day" pitch is a denominator trick. Re-anchor it to annual % and the deal usually exposes itself. A "0.6%/month" installment is often 13%+ APR.
    _("每天只要 X 块"是分母障眼法,换成年化就现形。)_

15. AI won't replace you. But someone using AI will replace someone who refuses to. I'd rather be early and a little embarrassed than late.
    _(AI 不会淘汰你,但会用 AI 的人会淘汰拒绝用的人。)_
